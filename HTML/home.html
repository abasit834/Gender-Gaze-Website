Welcome Note

Welcome to Gender Gaze: Reframing the Pakistani Narrative

Dear Readers,

Welcome to a journey of introspection, transformation, and empowerment. "Gender Gaze: Reframing the Pakistani Narrative" is more than just a magazine; it's a movement, a platform, and a celebration of diverse voices coming together to reshape the way we perceive and navigate gender in Pakistan.

In a society rich with history and culture, we recognize the power and potential of rewriting the narrative that surrounds gender. We believe in challenging norms, shedding light on untold stories, and elevating voices that have long been marginalized. This magazine is our canvas, and each article, interview, and piece of artwork is a stroke toward a more equitable and inclusive future.

Through the lens of gender, we explore the complexities and nuances of Pakistani society. We celebrate the achievements and resilience of women and individuals from marginalized genders who continue to defy expectations. We invite you to join us in questioning stereotypes, sharing your experiences, and contributing to a dialogue that is both meaningful and essential.

Here, you will find thought-provoking articles that delve into the heart of gender issues, uplifting stories that showcase the power of empowerment, and human narratives that reflect the fresh perspectives on living, eating, and being happy. We are committed to providing a space for open discussion and engagement, where every voice is valued and heard.

As we embark on this journey together, we extend our gratitude to our contributors, readers, and supporters who make this magazine possible. Your voices and stories fuel this movement, and your engagement with our community is a testament to the importance of these conversations.

So, welcome to the inaugural issue of "Gender Gaze: Reframing the Pakistani Narrative." Join us in embracing the challenge of reframing gender narratives and imagining a future where gender equality and equity in Pakistan are not just ideals but a lived reality.

Thank you for being part of this transformative journey.

With warmth and inspiration,

Sahirish Kashif
Editor-in-Chief
