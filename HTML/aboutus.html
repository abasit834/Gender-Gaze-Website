About Us

Mission

At "Gender Gaze: Reframing the Pakistani Narrative," our mission is to ignite conversations, challenge preconceptions, and inspire action towards a more inclusive and equitable Pakistan. We are committed to providing a dynamic platform for critical discourse, personal stories, and creative expression centered around gender issues and social narratives.

Vision

Our vision is a Pakistan where gender equality is not a distant dream but an everyday reality. We envision a society where individuals of all genders are free to pursue their aspirations, unrestricted by stereotypes and bias. We aim to foster a culture of empathy, empowerment, and change by amplifying voices that are often silenced and spotlighting stories that deserve recognition.

Through thoughtful articles, inspiring interviews, artistic expression, and a touch of humor, we strive to reshape the narrative surrounding gender. We envision a future where our magazine serves as a catalyst for transformation, forging a path towards a more just, inclusive, and harmonious Pakistan.

Join us on this remarkable journey of reflection, exploration, and empowerment as we cast a new "Gender Gaze" on the Pakistani narrative and together, create a brighter, more equitable tomorrow.
